WILD KARNATAKA

Explore Karnataka

The wild world of ‘One State Many Worlds’ is something that entices all age groups. Travellers, photographers, nature and wildlife lovers never leave any opportunities to soak up in the lush and rustic greenery. Karnataka is home to numerous wildlife and bird sanctuaries and a jungle safari is something that every traveller craves. Blessed with some rare and endangered species, wildlife in Karnataka is one of the most sought-after holidays. With almost 20% of the forest area, Karnataka shelters wild animals like Black Panther, leopards, tigers, elephants, sloth bears, and many more. Karnataka has more than 20 wildlife sanctuaries and attracts tourists from across the globe.
